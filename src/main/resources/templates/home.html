Tips to improve your financial health

Save Smart

Aim to save at least 20% of your income each month. Track your expenses regularly to identify areas where you can cut back.

Budget Regularly

Create a monthly budget and review it weekly. Categorize your spending to understand where your money goes and make informed decisions.

Emergency Fund

Build an emergency fund covering 3-6 months of expenses. Start small and gradually increase your savings for unexpected financial situations.

Monitor Spending

Keep track of all your transactions. Regular monitoring helps you stay within budget and achieve your financial goals faster.
